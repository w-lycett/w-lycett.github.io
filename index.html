As a Computer Science undergraduate student at Keele University, I am acutely aware of the need for agile problem-solving and critical thinking in the modern computing industry. With new technologies arriving at rapid rates each year, those who work with them must be adaptable and flexible; traits I strive to display in all facets of my professional career.
